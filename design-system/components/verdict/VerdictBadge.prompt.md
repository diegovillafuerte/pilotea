The traffic-light verdict pill — the core signal of the product. Colour does the talking; the word names it.

```jsx
<VerdictBadge level="green" />   {/* Verde — conviene */}
<VerdictBadge level="yellow" />  {/* Amarillo — regular */}
<VerdictBadge level="red" />     {/* Rojo — no conviene */}
```

Three levels only. Never use verde/amarillo/rojo decoratively elsewhere — they mean "verdict". Pair colour with text wherever it's the only signal (accessibility rule).
