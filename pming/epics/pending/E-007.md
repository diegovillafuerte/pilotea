# Benchmarks, Compare & Monetization

The paid layer: city percentile benchmarks (ported engine + synthetic seeds), Uber-vs-DiDi comparison from auto-captured data, rule-based recommendations, and Google Play Billing subscriptions. Reader stays free; this is what we sell after the hook lands. Pricing anchored by competitors (GigU $6.90 USD, StopClub R$9.90–39.90): free trial without card, ~$59–99 MXN/mo tiers.

**Reopened 2026-06-16** for the Comparar redesign (S-024): the founder revisited the original vision — drivers comparing themselves **against the population in percentiles** (plus platform averages) per metric and week. This reframes the Compare surface from cross-platform paired bars into a weekly benchmarking hub, and expands the backend benchmarks (net_earnings + a combined "all" population). Spec: `docs/comparar-redesign-spec.md`.

## Stories
- S-017: Percentiles & compare (done)
- S-018: Subscriptions (done)
- S-024: Comparar redesign — weekly benchmarking hub (the percentile-vs-city rebuild + branded shareable hero)

## Success criteria
- Driver sees their percentile per metric vs their city (national fallback) on real captured data
- Cross-platform compare identifies the winner per metric (commission inverted)
- Trial → paid conversion flow works end-to-end through Play Billing
- Free tier keeps reader + basic day stats; premium gates benchmarks/compare/history
- Comparar shows Tú (blended) vs each platform's city average vs the driver's percentile, branded and shareable (S-024)
