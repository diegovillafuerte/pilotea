# Monetization & Fiscal

Introduces the freemium paywall (Pro $59/mo, Multi $99/mo), payment processing, and fiscal reports for tax declarations.

Phase 3 of the technical design. Depends on E-002 (comparison is the key paid feature behind the Multi paywall).

## Stories
- S-007: Subscriptions & Paywall
- S-008: Fiscal Reports

## Success criteria
- Payment flow works end-to-end (subscribe, manage, cancel)
- Compare page gated behind Multi tier (client + server enforcement)
- Recommendations gated behind Pro tier
- Fiscal page shows monthly earnings breakdown by platform
- PDF download works for accountant handoff
