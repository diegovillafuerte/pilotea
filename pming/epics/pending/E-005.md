# Android Foundation & Realtime Reader

The ground-up rebuild: native Kotlin/Compose Android app whose hook feature reads Uber and DiDi trip-offer cards in real time (AccessibilityService) and overlays an instant profitability verdict ($/km, $/min, net profit, traffic light) before the driver accepts. This is the free download hook. Design per `docs/android-technical-design.md`; competitive grounding per `docs/competitive-analysis.md`.

## Stories
- S-011: Project scaffolding & architecture
- S-012: Capture engine & overlay
- S-013: Onboarding, permissions & compliance

## Success criteria
- Driver installs from Play, completes accessibility onboarding, and sees correct verdicts on live Uber and DiDi offer cards in CDMX
- Event → overlay latency < 150 ms; parse success rate > 95% on current host-app versions
- Parser specs updateable via remote config without a Play release
- Fully on-device processing; read-only (no host-app automation)
