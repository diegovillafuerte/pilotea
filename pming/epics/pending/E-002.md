# Multi-Platform Support

Adds the remaining 3 parsers (Uber screenshot, DiDi, InDrive) and the cross-platform comparison experience. Also introduces the recommendation engine and streak tracking.

Phase 2 of the technical design. Depends on E-001 being complete.

## Stories
- S-005: Additional Platform Parsers
- S-006: Cross-Platform Comparison & Recommendations

## Success criteria
- All 4 parser types work (Uber PDF, Uber screenshot, DiDi 2-image, InDrive)
- Driver using multiple platforms sees side-by-side comparison
- Recommendation engine generates actionable insights post-upload
- Streak counter tracks consecutive weeks of uploads
