# Fiscal & IMSS (Reform Features)

Uniquely Mexican differentiation no competitor has: the 2025 platform-work reform (in force 2025-06-22; IMSS pilot rules 2025-07-01) ties social-security coverage to earning ≥ 1 monthly minimum wage (~MXN $8,364) per platform. Kompara tracks each driver's monthly net per platform against the threshold, explains coverage status, and generates fiscal summaries under the unchanged LISR platform regime.

## Stories
- S-019: IMSS & fiscal tracking

## Success criteria
- Driver sees live progress toward the IMSS threshold per platform per month
- Plain-Spanish explainers for coverage, withholding, and open regulatory questions
- Exportable monthly fiscal summary (PDF)
