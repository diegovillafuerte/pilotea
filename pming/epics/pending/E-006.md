# Driver Stats & Data Platform

Turn captured offers/trips into the driver's automatic earnings ledger: shift/day/week stats with a real cost profile (net, not gross), plus the greenfield thin backend (WhatsApp auth, consented aggregate sync, benchmarks delivery) and the upload-import port of the web MVP (Claude Vision parsing of Uber PDF / screenshots) for backfill and platforms not yet captured live.

## Stories
- S-014: Trips, shifts & costs
- S-015: Accounts & greenfield backend
- S-016: Upload import (web-MVP port)

## Success criteria
- A week of driving produces a complete weekly summary with zero manual input
- Net earnings reflect the driver's own cost profile (fuel, maintenance, insurance, rent)
- App fully usable anonymously; account only needed for sync/benchmarks/premium
- Weekly Uber PDF import produces the same weekly_data shape as live capture
