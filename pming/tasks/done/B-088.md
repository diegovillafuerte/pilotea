# Comparar — "Dónde puedes ganar más" comparison opportunities

The bottom section of Comparar: 1–3 opportunity cards derived **from the comparison** (not the driver's own best-moments — those live on Inicio). See `docs/comparar-redesign-spec.md` §5.

## What to do
1. **`ComparisonOpportunities`** (new pure builder) — emits `Recommendation`-shaped items from the blended `PeriodStats`, the platform `PopulationStat`s, and the percentiles:
   - **Platform-mix:** compare platform `mean` for a lead metric + the driver's app mix (hours per platform) → "Los choferes ganan {gap}% más con {plataforma} — tú manejas más {otra}." (fires when better-platform mean ≥ 15% higher and the driver under-indexes there).
   - **Top-of-city gap:** `PopulationStat.p90` vs the driver's value → "El top 20% gana ${gap}/h más que tú."
   - **Take-rate standing:** driver commission vs Uber `mean` → "Tu comisión ({x}%) es mejor que el promedio ({y}%)." (only when commission present).
2. **Render** with `RecommendationCard(type, title, body)` (POSITIVE→VerdictGreen, INFO→INFO_BLUE). Cap 3, sort by priority. Spanish copy in code.
3. **Gating:** these render inside Comparar's `GateSurface.COMPARE` gate (B-087); feed the builder empty inputs when locked so nothing leaks.

## Acceptance criteria
- 1–3 relevant comparison opportunities from real weekly data; none fire on insufficient data.
- Nothing composed when COMPARE is locked.
- Mexican Spanish; reuses `RecommendationCard`.

## Dependencies
- B-086 (comparison model). Renders inside B-087's gate. Ref spec §5.

## Result (done 2026-06-16, branch `feat/comparar-redesign`)
- New pure `ComparisonOpportunities.kt`: platform-mix gap (≥15% + under-indexed), single-app 'prueba la otra app', top-of-city p90 gap. Emits `Recommendation`s rendered via `RecommendationCard` inside the COMPARE gate. Wired into the VM (app mix from week rows).
- `ComparisonOpportunitiesTest` (4 rules) green.
