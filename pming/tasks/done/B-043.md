# Aggregate sync & benchmarks delivery

The consented data exchange: weekly aggregates up (builds our real population data), city benchmarks down (powers percentiles). Raw capture data never leaves the device.

## What to do

1. `:sync` — WorkManager-scheduled sync: upsert weekly aggregates (driver × platform × week) for consented, signed-in drivers; exponential backoff, offline-tolerant
2. Explicit consent toggle ("Compartir mis promedios semanales de forma anónima para comparativas") — default off until onboarding asks
3. Benchmarks download: population_stats per city × platform × metric, cached locally with TTL
4. Backend: ingestion endpoint with validation; periodic job folds real aggregates into population_stats as sample sizes grow (replacing synthetic seeds city by city)
5. Conflict rules: imported (B-045) vs captured weeks — captured wins, both flagged

## Acceptance criteria
- Sync payload contains only derived aggregates (asserted in tests)
- Benchmarks available offline after first fetch
- Real-data fold-in raises sample_size and switches off synthetic flag per cell
