# Comparar by day & hour — DEFERRED

The session explored a Semana/Día/Hora zoom and a day×franja "you vs. the city" heatmap (`.session-captures/comparar/` v3/v4 mockups), but it's **blocked on data**: the city averages and percentiles are weekly/city-level only, and the driver's own hourly data exists only for Uber (the weekly PDF) or later if per-trip data can be stored legally. Day-specific comparison ("otros ganan más los domingos") needs day-of-week + time-band population benchmarks per platform, which don't exist.

Keep this in the backlog until that data lands. See `docs/comparar-redesign-spec.md` §13 (Phase 3).

## What to do (when unblocked)
1. Add day-of-week (and later time-band) population benchmarks per platform to the backend.
2. Add the Semana/Día/Hora segmented zoom over the benchmark table + the "tú vs. la ciudad" comparison heatmap (green = les ganas, red = oportunidad).
3. Keep individual best-day/best-hour insights on **Inicio** (IA principle), not here.

## Acceptance criteria (deferred)
- Only start when day/hour population benchmark data exists for at least one platform.

## Dependencies
- B-087 (the weekly Comparar shell to extend). Blocked on daily/hourly benchmark data. Ref spec §13.

## Resolution — CANCELLED (2026-06-16)
Closed during the S-024 implementation pass (B-085–B-089 shipped on `feat/comparar-redesign`). **Not buildable now, by two hard constraints:**
1. **No data.** There are no daily/hourly population benchmarks (the engine is weekly/city only), and no daily/hourly driver data for most cases — only Uber's weekly PDF, or future legal per-trip capture.
2. **IA boundary.** A daily breakdown of the driver's *own* earnings belongs on **Inicio**, not Comparar (the locked IA principle: Inicio = individual, Comparar = comparison). A comparison-by-day in Comparar specifically needs day-level *population* benchmarks, which don't exist.

Cancelled rather than left open so the S-024 scope is terminal and honest. The design is preserved: the Semana/Día/Hora zoom + the "tú vs. la ciudad" day×franja heatmap are captured in the v3/v4 mockups (`.session-captures/comparar/`) and `docs/comparar-redesign-spec.md` §13 (Phase 3). The weekly Comparar shell (B-087) already carries a "Pronto: cómo te comparas por día y por hora" affordance and is structured to extend.

**Re-create a fresh task when the data lands** (day-of-week, then time-band, population benchmarks per platform — e.g. via Uber-PDF day/hour extraction or consented per-trip capture).
