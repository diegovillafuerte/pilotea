# Play Billing integration

Google Play Billing subscriptions: the competitor-standard motion (free trial without card → cheap monthly sub).

## What to do

1. `:billing` — Play Billing Library: monthly subscription product(s); offer with free-trial phase (trial without card per Play subs config)
2. Tiers: Premium (~$59–99 MXN/mo launch anchor — final pricing configured in Play Console + remote config, not hardcoded)
3. Purchase, restore, cancel, grace-period and account-hold states; acknowledge purchases correctly
4. Backend: subscription state sync via Play Developer API / RTDN webhook into B-041 schema
5. Test-track validation across the full lifecycle

## Acceptance criteria
- Full lifecycle (trial → active → cancel → restore) verified on internal track
- Entitlement state consistent across reinstall + device change (account-linked)
- No price strings hardcoded in the app

## Dependencies
- B-025 (architecture); B-042 helps for account linking (anonymous entitlements degrade gracefully)
