# Paywall & tier enforcement

The business model: reader free forever (the hook), analytics premium (the upsell) — enforced consistently.

## What to do

1. Entitlement gate component (port PaywallGate concept): free = reader + today stats; premium = percentiles, compare, full history, fiscal, advanced recommendations
2. Paywall screen: value framing anchored on driver math ("si te ayuda a ganar $200 más a la semana, se paga solo"), trial CTA, restore link
3. Gating placement: tease-then-gate (show blurred/partial premium content, not walls of nothing)
4. Free-tier history limit (e.g., 2 weeks) with upgrade prompt
5. Remote-config kill switch for gates (launch promo flexibility)

## Acceptance criteria
- Reader and overlay NEVER gated (hook stays free)
- Entitlement changes apply without restart
- Gate analytics events (anonymized) for conversion tuning

## Dependencies
- B-049 (billing), B-046 (premium content to gate)
