# Onboarding flow

Post-auth onboarding: collect driver's name, city, and platform selection. Progressive 3-step flow.

## What to do

1. `src/app/onboarding/page.tsx` — 3-step progressive form:
   - Step 1: Name (text input)
   - Step 2: City (searchable dropdown with 40 cities from constants.ts)
   - Step 3: Platforms (multi-select: Uber, DiDi, InDrive)
2. `src/lib/constants.ts` — city list (40 cities with keys and display names, from tech design Appendix A), platform definitions
3. `src/app/api/drivers/onboarding/route.ts` — POST: validate inputs (Zod), update driver record (name, city, platforms, onboarding_completed=true), redirect to /dashboard
4. Privacy badge: "Tus datos, tu control" shown during onboarding
5. Mobile-first design: large touch targets, one step per screen view

## Acceptance criteria
- New driver completes 3 steps and lands on dashboard
- All 40 cities searchable and selectable
- Multi-platform selection works (1-3 platforms)
- Driver record updated with name, city, platforms
- onboarding_completed flag set to true
- Cannot skip steps — all 3 required
- Returning drivers (onboarding_completed=true) never see onboarding again
