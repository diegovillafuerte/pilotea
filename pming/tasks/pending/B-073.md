# Onboarding CTAs render under the gesture-nav inset

Found in the 2026-06-12 on-device session (Samsung S25, gesture navigation, edge-to-edge): the
bottom `PrimaryButton` on the onboarding screens draws underneath the system gesture area —
"Continuar"/"Empezar" show roughly half-height, the disclosure's "No acepto" link is clipped, and
taps near the bottom edge can be consumed by the system instead of the button.

## What to do
1. Apply window insets (`safeDrawingPadding()` / `navigationBarsPadding()`) to the onboarding
   screen containers (PitchScreen, DisclosureScreen, LimitedInfo, AccessibilityGrant, OEM, Done)
   and the signup flow's `StepScaffold` — likely one shared fix at the funnel root.
2. Audit the other full-screen surfaces (paywall, import, share card) for the same pattern.

## Acceptance criteria
- All onboarding/signup CTAs fully visible above the gesture bar on a gesture-nav device
- Evidence: re-run Suite A of `docs/didi-test-plan.md` — buttons fully visible in screenshots

## Result

Fixed at the funnel root in `android/ui/src/main/java/mx/kompara/ui/nav/KomparaRoot.kt`: the
`ONBOARDING` and `AUTH` branches now wrap their content in `Modifier.safeDrawingPadding()`. This is
one shared fix covering every onboarding screen (PitchScreen, DisclosureScreen, LimitedInfo,
AccessibilityGrant, OEM, Done) and the whole signup `StepScaffold`, since both funnels render
directly inside `KomparaRoot`'s edge-to-edge `Surface` with no system-bar handling of their own.
`safeDrawingPadding()` insets for the status bar, gesture/nav bar, and display cutouts, so the bottom
CTAs ("Continuar"/"Empezar", the disclosure's "No acepto" link) now sit fully above the gesture area
and top titles clear the status bar.

Audit of the other full-screen surfaces (step 2): **no fix needed.** The paywall, import, and
share-card screens are reached inside `KomparaApp`'s `Scaffold`, whose `innerPadding` already accounts
for the bottom bar — and `KomparaBottomBar` itself consumes the `navigationBars` inset
(`KomparaBottomBar.kt`). The `MAIN` branch is therefore deliberately excluded from the root-level
padding to avoid double-insetting.

Verification: `./gradlew :ui:compileDebugKotlin`, `:ui:testDebugUnitTest`, and `:app:assembleDebug`
all pass. `pnpm lint` clean; `pnpm test` shows only the 24 pre-existing legacy-web baseline failures
(no new failures — change is Android-only).

On-device Suite A re-verification (Samsung S25, gesture nav) is **pending the next device session** —
no physical gesture-nav device was available in this run.
