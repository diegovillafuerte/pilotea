# Driver beta program

Real CDMX drivers on the closed track, feeding parser fixtures and product feedback before public launch. Human task.

## What to do

1. Recruit 20–30 drivers (CDMX first): driver Facebook/WhatsApp groups, personal network, small incentive (lifetime premium for founding testers)
2. Founding-drivers WhatsApp group: announcements, bug reports, fixture requests ("¿alguien con inDrive nos manda una oferta no leída?")
3. Weekly feedback cadence: parse success review (B-034 telemetry), top friction, NPS pulse
4. Beta exit criteria definition: parse success > 95% across both platforms for 2 consecutive weeks, onboarding completion > 70%, no critical bugs
5. Capture testimonials for the public listing

## Acceptance criteria
- ≥ 20 active beta drivers across Uber + DiDi
- Fixture/feedback loop demonstrably improving parse rates
- Exit criteria met → go/no-go documented for public launch
