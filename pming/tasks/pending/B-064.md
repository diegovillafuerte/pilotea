# inDrive MX OCR spec (real captures)

Author the inDrive OCR parser spec from real captured bid cards (list/"Pon Tu Precio" model).

## What to do
1. Capture real inDrive offer/bid OCR blocks on-device
2. Spec: bid amount, pickup/trip distance, duration, rating; list of simultaneous bids
3. Fixture corpus from real captures (≥15)

## Acceptance criteria
- ≥95% parse on the real-capture corpus
- Verdict chip appears over real inDrive bids

## ⏳ OFFLINE — owner action (blocks all of the above)

(OCR→verdict routing) is **done**, so the chip will light once the parser lands.
