# Fiscal Reports

Monthly earnings breakdown page and downloadable PDF for the driver's accountant. Simple format — earnings by platform, trips, commissions, totals.

## Tasks
- B-018: Fiscal page and PDF generation

## Acceptance criteria
- Fiscal page shows month selector and earnings breakdown by platform
- Shows: net earnings, gross earnings, total trips, commissions, taxes per platform
- Monthly totals across all platforms
- "Descargar PDF" button generates a clean PDF
- PDF suitable for handing to an accountant for RESICO declarations
- Works with whatever data the driver has uploaded (handles partial months)
