# Subscriptions & Paywall

Payment processing, subscription management, and paywall enforcement for Pro ($59 MXN/mo) and Multi ($99 MXN/mo) tiers.

## Tasks
- B-016: Payment integration and subscription management
- B-017: Paywall enforcement

## Acceptance criteria
- Drivers can subscribe to Pro or Multi via payment processor
- Subscription status tracked in DB (active, cancelled, expired)
- Compare page gated behind Multi tier
- Personalized recommendations gated behind Pro tier
- Paywall enforced both client-side (PaywallGate component) and server-side (API checks)
- Upgrade prompts shown at appropriate moments in free tier
- Key principle: cross-platform comparison is ALWAYS paid (never free, so no perceived loss)
