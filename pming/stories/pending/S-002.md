# Auth & Onboarding

WhatsApp magic link authentication and the post-auth onboarding flow where drivers enter their name, city, and platforms.

## Tasks
- B-004: WhatsApp magic link auth
- B-005: Onboarding flow

## Acceptance criteria
- Driver enters phone number → receives WhatsApp message with magic link
- Clicking magic link logs them in and sets session cookie
- New drivers redirected to onboarding; returning drivers go to dashboard
- Onboarding collects name, city (40-city picker), and platform selection
- Session persists for 30 days
- Rate limiting: max 3 magic links per phone per 15 minutes
