# Comparar redesign — weekly benchmarking hub

Rebuild the `Comparar` tab around the original product vision: the driver compares **against the rest of the population in percentiles**, plus the platform averages, per metric and per week. Replaces today's cross-platform paired-bars screen.

Full spec: `docs/comparar-redesign-spec.md` (data sources, ViewModel changes, composables, gating, strings, tests, phasing). Design mockups + ground-truth brief in `.session-captures/comparar/`. Decided 2026-06-16.

**IA principle (durable):** individual earnings → Inicio; comparison vs. other drivers → Comparar. "Best day / best hours" stays on Inicio.

**Shape:** a branded, shareable percentile hero (free) over a weekly benchmark TABLE — per metric: **Tú** (blended across apps) · **Uber** avg · **DiDi** avg (city population means) · **Tu lugar** (percentile vs all drivers) — plus a "Dónde puedes ganar más" opportunities section. Branding (Kompara K + wordmark) rides every screen; the hero doubles as the WhatsApp share card. Weekly only — day/hour is deferred (B-090) until that data exists.
