# Trips, shifts & costs

Auto-build the driver's earnings ledger from captured data: trip log, shift/day/week rollups, goals and streaks, and the cost profile that turns gross into net everywhere in the app.

- Today/week dashboards render captured data with Spanish copy, MXN formatting
