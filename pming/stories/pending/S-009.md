# Viral & Sharing Features

Shareable monthly summary cards and WhatsApp-based notifications for retention.

## Tasks
- B-019: "Tu Mes" shareable card
- B-020: Weekly WhatsApp reminder

## Acceptance criteria
- "Tu Mes" generates a branded image card (Satori) with monthly stats
- Card includes: earnings, percentile, best day, best platform, streak
- "Compartir en WhatsApp" button shares card image + deep link
- Every shared card has Kompara branding (free marketing)
- Weekly cron sends WhatsApp message on Monday mornings: "Como te fue? Sube tus datos"
- Reminder only sent to drivers who uploaded in the last 4 weeks (active users)
